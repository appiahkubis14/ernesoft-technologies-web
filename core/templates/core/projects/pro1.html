International AI-Powered Agriculture Intelligence Platform

AfariSense represents a groundbreaking international collaboration between Ghanaian and Senegalese agritech companies, delivering comprehensive agricultural intelligence through advanced AI and machine learning technologies.

The platform combines computer vision, predictive analytics, and hyperlocal weather forecasting to empower farmers across West Africa with real-time insights and actionable recommendations for improved crop management and increased productivity.

Project Challenges & Solutions

The agricultural sector in West Africa faces significant challenges including crop diseases, pest infestations, unpredictable weather patterns, and limited market access. AfariSense addresses these through a comprehensive suite of technological solutions that bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern digital agriculture.

Key Features & Technologies

  • AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection (92% Accuracy)
  • Predictive Yield Forecasting
  • Hyperlocal Weather Prediction
  • Cross-Border E-commerce Marketplace
  • Multi-Language Support (French, English, Wolof)
  • Mobile Money Integration
  • Real-time Alert System
Transforming West African Agriculture Through Intelligent Technology Solutions

The platform's microservices architecture deployed on AWS ensures high availability and scalability, serving diverse user segments from smallholder farmers to commercial agribusinesses. The comprehensive digital profiling system with GIS land mapping provides unprecedented visibility into farming operations across the region.

Technologies Used

  • Django Backend
  • TensorFlow AI
  • AWS Cloud
  • React Native Mobile
  • PostgreSQL Database
