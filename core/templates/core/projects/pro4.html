Child Labour Monitoring & Remediation Platform

This comprehensive human rights monitoring system addresses critical challenges in cocoa supply chains by providing tools for identifying, tracking, and remediating child labour incidents while ensuring compliance with international standards.

The platform combines farmer registration, family profiling, and GIS-enabled mapping to create a transparent monitoring framework that supports ethical sourcing and sustainable agricultural practices.

Ethical Supply Chain Management

Monitoring human rights compliance in complex agricultural supply chains requires sophisticated tools for data collection, risk assessment, and incident management. Our system provides field agents with offline-capable data collection tools while maintaining secure handling of sensitive personal information.

Platform Capabilities

  • Farmer Registration & Family Profiling
  • Child Labour Risk Assessment
  • GIS-Enabled Community Mapping
  • Incident Tracking & Remediation
  • Automated Compliance Reporting
  • Third-party Certification Integration
  • Offline Data Collection Support
Promoting Ethical Cocoa Production Through Technology

The system's sophisticated alert management with escalation workflows ensures timely response to identified cases, while comprehensive analytics provide insights into program effectiveness and compliance metrics across the supply chain.
