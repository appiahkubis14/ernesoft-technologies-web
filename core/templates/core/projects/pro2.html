Ghana's Premier Cocoa Supply Chain Traceability Platform

EduR Cocoa represents a comprehensive traceability solution designed to track cocoa from farm to export, ensuring transparency and compliance throughout Ghana's cocoa supply chain.

The platform integrates with Ghana Cocoa Board systems to provide real-time monitoring of cocoa production, certification, and export processes, supporting the country's position as a leader in sustainable cocoa production.

Supply Chain Transparency Challenges

The cocoa industry faces significant challenges in tracking produce from smallholder farms through complex supply chains to international markets. EduR Cocoa addresses these through robust database design and seamless integration with existing regulatory systems.

Platform Capabilities

  • End-to-End Supply Chain Tracking
  • Geospatial Farm Mapping
  • Regulatory Compliance Integration
  • Real-time Certification Monitoring
  • Production Analytics & Reporting
  • Mobile Data Collection
  • Export Documentation Automation
Ensuring Sustainable Cocoa Production Through Digital Traceability

The system's robust PostgreSQL database schema efficiently handles complex supply chain relationships while maintaining data integrity. RESTful APIs support both mobile and web interfaces, enabling field agents and administrators to access real-time information from anywhere.
