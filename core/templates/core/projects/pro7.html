Hybrid Community-based Monitoring System (HCMS)

A comprehensive environmental monitoring solution for the Sui River cocoa-forest landscape in Ghana's Western North Region, covering 333.9 km² of forest reserve area.

The system employs a landscape participatory approach to secure the future of forests while significantly enhancing livelihood opportunities for farmers and forest users through advanced technology integration.

Sustainable Forest Management

HCMS leverages information and communications technology to empower local communities in environmental monitoring while minimizing human error. The system follows the Bia-Tano watershed, south of Buaku, focusing on sustainable forest management and livelihood improvement for local communities through real-time data collection and analysis.

System Features

  • Real-time Environmental Monitoring
  • Community Participation Platform
  • GIS-based Mapping & Analysis
  • Mobile Data Collection
  • Deforestation Alert System
  • Livelihood Impact Assessment
  • Multi-stakeholder Dashboard
Empowering Communities Through Technology

The platform's sophisticated monitoring capabilities with escalation workflows ensure timely response to environmental threats, while comprehensive analytics provide insights into forest conservation effectiveness and community impact metrics across the monitored landscape.

Technology Stack

  • Geospatial Analysis
  • Mobile Applications
  • Cloud Computing
  • GIS Mapping
  • Data Analytics
